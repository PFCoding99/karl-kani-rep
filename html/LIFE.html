LIFE COLLECTION

Introducing the Karl Kani Fall ‘22 collection, a tribute to the original Brooklyn, NY aesthetics still driving the future of streetwear. Signature styles from the 90’s are reimagined and accompanied by expertly crafted wardrobe staples and statement pieces. Butter-soft lambskins and ring-spun vintage washed cottons are complemented by a versatile neutral palette and finished with luxurious leather, rubber or embroidered logo detailing on each design.
